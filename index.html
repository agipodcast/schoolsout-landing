Why this exists

Educators are being handed tools before they have a map.

The AI conversation in education is moving fast. Students are testing tools, teachers are setting rules class by class, and schools are trying to make policy while the ground keeps moving. That is a hard place to teach from.

Schools Out Saturdays brings real conversations to the surface. Teachers, faculty, founders, workforce people, and school leaders talking about what they are seeing, what they are worried about, and what they wish more people understood. No canned ed-tech pitch. No magic wand.

Schools Out Field Notes adds a weekly rhythm to that conversation. One practical signal, one small thing to think about, and one reply question that helps us learn from the people closest to students.
