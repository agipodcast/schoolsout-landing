Teaser copy:
I spent part of this weekend trying to get multiple AI agents to collaborate in one shared room.

Here is the thing I learned fast:

One AI agent feels like magic.

Three AI agents feel like management.

The issue was not whether the models were smart. They were.

The issue was ownership.

Who decides?
Where is the record?
What counts as done?
Who gets to touch the browser?
What receipt proves the work happened?

That is the part the demos skip.
